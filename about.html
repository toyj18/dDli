About us

We are digital transformation consultants. Our team is passionate about technology and we believe in its power to transform the way people work. Through intelligent process automation, we help businesses achieve their goals.

A Brief History

dipoleDIAMOND was incorporated in July 2009. It will however not be until March 2015 before full business operations started. The business started by building websites and providing IT advisory for small businesses. By the end of 2015, dipoleDIAMOND had hired it’s first full-time employee, become a Kentico Partner, and helped in the digital transformation of Nigeria’s foremost mathematics quiz competition for secondary schools.

In 2016, the company added more clients, implemented an intranet solution for one of Nigeria’s Power Generating Companies and became profitable. The following year, dipoleDIAMOND became a ProcessMaker Elite Partner and won our first major BPM client – a Merchant Bank. This was the first of many, and since then, we have helped financial institutions and other businesses in East and West Africa automate their business processes, design and implement bespoke solutions, helping them accelerate their digital transformation journeys.

The team has also grown with talents from diverse backgrounds such as Banking, Engineering and Law. Our commitment to help our customers solve business problems with today’s technology and nurture the best tech talent is what drives us daily.
We recently partnered with UiPath to provide RPA solutions and started fofx Academy – a school for digital skills.
